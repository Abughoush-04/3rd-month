Our First Three Months

Three months.
Three chapters.
One story I still choose every single day.

Month One — The Beginning

Everything was new. Curious. Soft in a way that didn’t need explanations.

We were learning each other’s voices, rhythms, and silences. Every small moment felt like magic.

Month Two — Cozy Moments

This month had lots of warmth. Moments that made the world stop for us.

Remember when we got stuck in the car during the rain? And we watched that movie together while the rain hit the windows? I’ll never forget how calm and happy we felt, just being us.

Month Three — Adventures

This month we had so much fun. So many adventures and laughter that made every day feel alive.

And yes… the past few days were hard. But even through the tension, I saw how much we care and how deep this connection goes.

Every month has its moments, soft, cozy, wild. And I choose you through all of them.
